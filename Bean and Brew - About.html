Welcome to Bean and Brew, a coffee shop and bakery chain that provides you with excellent food and drink, exactly how you want it.

We are a small chain of cafe's, coffee shops and bakeries based around the Harrogate and Leeds areas.

We prioritise local comfort and a calm atmosphere in the shop, so that you can have an interesting and relaxing time with us, where you can meet with friends or rest alone.

We are proud to be one of the first companies in the UK to use both fair-trade coffee and all organic milk in our products.

This provides a delicious taste to our drinks, while giving back to the people that grew the ingredients and helping support the health of our patrons.
